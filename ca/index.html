About me

My name is Argyris Synanis. I am a Creative Developer focusing on Responsive Web Design and Web development.


I am currently working as a full-time Developer at the award winning ReallyB2B since June 2017. My main responsibility in the agency is the development of responsive emails and landing pages in a variety of platforms such as Marketo, Adestra, Hubspot, and Unbounce.

Also, I am responsible for the development of Motion Graphics straight from a flat design. During my time in the Agency and working closely with the Creative Team, I have enhanced my skills in the Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and After Effects.

When I am not writing code or animating infographics, you will find me training with the Southsea rowing club on the seafront.
